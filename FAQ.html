FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS/RINK PACKAGES

General Rink Questions

 
Q: How do we buy skate tickets for General Admission?
A: General Admission skating is on a first come, first serve basis. If there is a line, everyone in your party must wait in line together to purchase tickets. Our peak holiday season is from Friday November 7th through January 6th and during this time period, lines are likely to be long for all sessions. During the busy holiday season you should anticipate an approximate one hour wait. Please note that you do wait outdoors. We recommend coming for our early 8:30 am – 10 am session if possible - lines are typically shortest first thing in the morning. To avoid the lines completely, you may now make reservations to skate through our VIP SKATE or FIRST SKATE packages.

Q: When is the Rink open?
A: We are open daily for 90 minute sessions. 8:30-10:00AM, 10:30AM-12:00PM, 12:30-2:00PM, 2:30-4:00PM, 4:30-6:00PM, 6:30-8:00PM, 8:30-10:00PM, and 10:30PM-12:00AM.

Q: Do you have group rates?
A: We do have group rates if you can join us anytime between opening day and November 6th or from January 7th through the day we close the rink for the season.

We do NOT offer group rates in our peak holiday season: Friday November 7th through January 6th.

Rates for group skating are: Adults: $15, Children: $10, Skate Rental: $8. This requires a 15 person minimum. Please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com to make your booking.

Q: Do you have Skating Lessons?
A: Private lesson are available at a cost of $50.00 for 30 minutes of private instructions plus the full session of skating. Skate rentals, if needed, are an additional $12.00 charge. Reservations are recommended. Please email therink@patinagroup.com to reserve a skating lesson.

Q: Can I bring my own skates?
A: Yes, but speed skates are not allowed.

Q: Do your skates run big or small?
A: Skates are whole sizes only, true to size. Just let us know your regular shoe size, we know how to determine the proper skate sizes.

Q: Is there an age limit to skate?
A: No, there is no age restrictions for skating. Children under 6 must be accompanied by an adult.

Q: Do you have skates for small kids? What is the biggest size?
A: Yes, our smallest skate is a baby 6 and our largest skate is men's 14.

Q: Can you get a season pass for the rink?
A: Yes, you can. (Season Pass with a locker $450, Season Pass Only $275, Locker Rental Only $175). To become a session pass holder, please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com.

Q: Can we have our child’s birthday party at the rink?
A: Yes, you can. We love birthday parties! Our birthday packages are available from opening day in October through November 6th and again, from January 7th until the day we close the rink for the season. We do not have these packages available during our peak holiday season from November 7th through January 6th.

To make your birthday party reservation or for more information, please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com.

Q: What if it rains or snows?
A: The Rink is open during all inclement weather conditions.

Q: Are toddlers free?
A: Admission is free for children under 4, but all skate rentals must be paid for, regardless of age.

Q: What is considered a senior citizen?
A: Senior citizens are 65 and up.

Q: Do you have figure skates and/or hockey skates?
A: We only have figure skates.

Q: What happens is there are only 15 minutes left in the skate session I purchased tickets for?
A: You can continue to skate in the next session. Just remember to keep your ticket and do not return your rented skates.

Q: Do you give a military or student discount?
A: We do not offer student discounts, but we are proud to offer military discounts to both active United States armed service personnel and all United States veterans. The discount is 15% for both General Admission skating and skate rentals. Please have your military ID and available for our cashiers when you are here purchasing tickets. ID must be presented and valid to receive the 15% discount.

Q: Who can I contact for press and media inquiries?
A: Please contact Shin-Jung Hong of Nicholas & Lence Communications with any media inquiries.

ENGAGEMENT ON ICE

 
Q: How do I get my tickets?
A: When your reservation is complete and we have received all your information, your tickets will be emailed to you.

Q: How should I get you my CD?
A: You can do this one of two ways: 1) Send it to us using any mail carrier service available that will provide you with a "proof of signature" option. (We recommend FedEx or UPS). This ensures that your magical song gets safely into our hands. Should your CD be lost in transit, we cannot accept responsibility for this and want to make this a truly special day for you. For this option, please send to: The Rink at Rock Center/General Manager Rockefeller Center Patina Restaurant Group 600 5th Avenue, Building 17, Office 6 New York, NY 10020 OR 2) You may ask for a RINK MANAGER at our General Admission skate house and hand it to them personally. Please ensure you ask a Manager as we need to secure it for you accordingly.

Q: What if there is a long line for skating?
A: Tickets for Engagement-On-Ice are uniquely designed for this package, and everyone who works at The Rink knows how to spot them. You will receive these tickets via email as well as instructions on how to bypass any waiting line.

Q: When exactly do I propose during my skating session?
A: Each skating session is 1 ½ hours long, and your proposal takes place at the end of your skate session. For example, if you book a 10:30 AM session, you will make your proposal at 12PM. In preparation for your proposal, we will take a few minutes to clear everyone from the ice (except you).

Q: How can I make sure my soon-to-be fiancé is completely surprised by my proposal?
A: When you confirm your reservation, we will send you a map for easy navigating, additional information and tips on how to drop off your CD, and choreograph your skate session and proposal all without your soon-to-be fiancé ever suspecting a thing.

Q: What if it rains or there is inclement weather?
A: The Rink remains open during all inclement weather conditions. If you would like to change or reschedule your special day far in advance, please let us know as soon as possible as many sessions become sold out. The engagement packages are very limited in regard to availability and we want to work with you as best as we can but are restricted in many cases.

Q: Will I get my money back if I cancel?
A: We can only issue refunds in the case of rain, and if you were unable to reschedule.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS/RINK PACKAGES

BREAKFAST WITH SANTA

 
Q: How long are Breakfast with Santa sessions? Are we able to arrive late?
A: Please be sure to arrive on time. We are unable to extend the reservation time for latecomers. Please note: Rock Center Café reservations have a duration of 1 hour and 15 minutes and The Sea Grill reservations have a duration of 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Q: Are reservations refundable?
A: Reservations are non-refundable and are only good for the date and time specified.

Q: If we have a large party, are we guaranteed to sit together?
A: Each of our special Breakfast with Santa sessions has a specific number of tables that are available for parties of 5 or more. This allows all of our guests to be accommodated. When you are booking on our webcart, your reservation maybe declined if the size of your request is too large of a group for that date. Please enter your second choice if that occurs.

Q: Can we have a window seat for our reservation?
A: We do not accept requests for specific tables in any of our locations at Rockefeller Center with the exception of our Engagement on Ice-Platinum Package. Our floor plans and ability to accommodate everyone does not allow us to take any requests for specific seating of any kind. For guests with wheel chairs and service pets, we will ensure you are accommodated as you arrive.

Q: Is skating included in our Breakfast with Santa reservation?
A: Your skating admission tickets will be held at the door on the day of your reservation and will be given to you upon presentation of your confirmation email.

Q: Do we need to use the skating tickets the day of our Breakfast with Santa reservation?
A: We recommend that you do. The skating tickets you receive when you arrive for Breakfast with Santa must be redeemed on that day OR you may return to skate after January 7th.

Q: Do we need to rent skates or can we bring personal skates?
A: You may do either. Skate rentals are available for $12.00 per person. Personal skates are permitted.

Q: Is there a Santa at both locations?
A: Yes, there will be a Santa at both Rock Center Café and The Sea Grill.

Q: What are the ages for children?
A: Children are ages 9 and under.

Q: If we do not want to skate, can we book for a lower price?
A: This package is only sold as a combination and no discounts can be applied if you decide to skip any of the offerings included.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Parking is located at 45 West 48th street. Rockefeller Center Parking Garage between 5th and 6th Avenues.
Guests for Breakfast with Santa only who enter by 9:00 AM will be allowed 6 hours maximum for $30.00.
Please pick up your voucher at the front desk on the day of event.
